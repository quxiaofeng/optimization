Question 2

  • least energy & error correction

Question 2: subj. to

Question 3

  • sparse regression with noise - lasso

Question 3: subj. to

  • 标准 lasso 问题
  • 标准的 SRC

Question 4

  • least energy with noise

Question 4: subj. to
